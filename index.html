The Bush Brothers

Bush Brothers Pavers specializes in the fusion of enduring Artistic Masonry and premium Paver Design, transforming exterior spaces across Manatee and Sarasota Counties.

For over 25 years, we’ve crafted custom hardscape features—from stunning paver driveways, patios, and pool decks to meticulously built masonry accents and benches.

Beyond installation, our services ensure the longevity of your investment through professional pressure washing and sealing to protect and enhance pavers, along with detailed general paver and driveway repairs to correct shifting or damaged units.

Furthermore, we install robust water management systems, including new pool drains, Deck-O-Drains, and comprehensive NDS drainage solutions featuring catch basins and Coraflex piping tied directly into downspouts, guaranteeing a beautiful, structurally sound, and well-drained property for years to come.
